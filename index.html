Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Here are the ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 100 g butter
  • 150 g brown sugar
  • 250 g flour
  • 1 unit baking powder
  • 1 unit vanilla extract

So here are the steps:<7b>

  1. melt the butter and mix with sugar
  2. then add eggs and mix them
  3. then add powder, vanilla and flour and mix
  4. bake in owen with 175 C for 12 minutes

andd.....IT IS READY!
